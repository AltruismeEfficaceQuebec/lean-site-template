Team


Sophie Pelland

A physicist by training, Sophie is a researcher at CanmetÉNERGIE (Natural Resources Canada); her research focuses on solar photovoltaic energy and the integration of renewable energy into electricity grids. Sophie is a member of the board of directors of Saint-Bruno en transition, a non-profit organization that aims to address climate change in the municipality of Saint-Bruno-de-Montarville. She is also a member of the [Ethical Investment Group]((https://sites.google.com/site/eiggiemtl/gie-eig), treasurer of Altruisme Efficace Québec and co-organizer of Altruisme Efficace Montréal. All the main cause areas of effective altruism interest her greatly.


Keven Bisson

Keven is a researcher on philosophy of longtermism.


Orpheus Lummis

Orpheus is interesting on reducing existential risks via AI safety, and also founder of AI Safety Events.
