Getting involved

  1. Attend our events. Bring friends!

  2. Follow the Effective Altruism Québec Facebook page. Consider joining the EA Montréal Facebook group, the EA Montŕeal Meetup group, the Altruisme Efficace Ville-de-Québec Facebook group, the EA McGill Facebook group, and the Altruisme Efficace UdeM (Université de Montréal) Facebook group.

  3. Read a book on the subject.

  4. Prepare an event, for example a presentation, and get in touch with us to make it happen!

  5. See effectivealtruism.org/get-involved for more great ideas!
